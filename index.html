Introduction

Greetings and welcome to my website! Depending on where you come from I have a few online names, most commonly I go by NightLights, and my pronouns are they/them. I have been practicing art for roughly 20+ and now have the courage to show it via this little website. I hope you enjoy! .

Background

I've always had a rather creative flair since I was a tiny tot, my family would tell stories of me coloring in every coloring book we had, and how I would fill all my schoolwork with doodles. As far as I can remember, art has always been in my life.

I started traditionally and transferred to digital in my later teens, now I focus on creating digital artwork. I've come a long way since doodling stickmen in my math book, and I know I will always have more to learn! Happy to have you all along for the journey!
